Mibena Italian Restaurant was founded by Grandma Mibena in 1940, when she migrated to Ireland
Mibena experienced continued success and expanded with a second location in Cork to match our expanding family
Enjoying a long and successful career, the founders retired and their eldest son Mario took over the family business
The family listened to their loyal customers and opened a third location in Galway
Continuing the family tradition, Mario's son took over the restaurant in 2005
